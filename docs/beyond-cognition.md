## What Is a Semantic Mind?

A **semantic mind** is not just an AI that chats or generates.  
It is an intelligence that understands structure, remembers with precision, and reasons with integrity.

It is:
- **Rooted in language** (via grammar and structure)

It doesn’t just simulate thought.
